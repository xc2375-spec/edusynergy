Lesson 4：你的大脑几乎预测你的一切 Lesson 4: Your Brain Predicts (Almost) Everything You Do

预测不是算命，而是大脑的默认工作方式：用过去来组织当下。 Prediction isn’t fortune-telling—it’s the brain’s default: using the past to organize the present.

概念卡Concept cards 讨论Discussion 练习Practice

Big Idea 核心概念Big Idea

你的体验（看到/听到/感觉到）并不是被动接收，而是大脑在做‘预测—校正’。 Experience isn’t passive input—it’s prediction with error-correction.

Concept Cards 概念卡Concept Cards

  • 预测（Prediction）：用过去经验生成对当下的‘最好猜测’。Prediction (预测): Generating best guesses from past experience.
  • 预测误差（Prediction error）：现实与预测不一致时的差值。Prediction error (预测误差): Mismatch between prediction and reality.
  • 学习（Learning）：通过误差更新模型。Learning (学习): Updating models through error.
  • 注意（Attention）：把资源分配给更重要的误差信号。Attention (注意): Allocating resources to important error signals.

理解展开Explanation

学习的本质是：允许出现预测误差，并在安全的条件下更新。‘不犯错’反而可能意味着任务太容易或太紧张。 Learning means allowing prediction errors and updating safely. ‘No mistakes’ can mean tasks are too easy—or anxiety is too high.

讨论问题Discussion

  • 你在什么情况下更愿意暴露错误？什么让你觉得安全？When are you more willing to show mistakes? What creates safety?
  • ‘我就是学不会’可能是哪个预测在作祟？What prediction might be behind “I can’t learn this”?
  • 老师如何设计“可承受的预测误差”？How can teachers design ‘tolerable prediction error’?

生活练习Practice

  • 本周刻意做“可控难度”：每次练习选一个略难点，允许错 1–2 次再纠正。Practice at “manageable difficulty”: allow 1–2 errors before correcting.
  • 写下一个旧预测：‘我不擅长口头表达’，并用数据反驳它（次数/时长/进步）。Write an old prediction (‘I’m bad at speaking’) and counter it with data (attempts/time/progress).
  • 做一次“解释给别人听”：把理解变成输出，暴露误差。Explain to someone else: output reveals hidden prediction errors.

教学应用（SEL × 学术）Teaching Application (SEL × Academics)

  • 建立“错误文化”：把错误分类（概念/计算/表达），并给每类一个修复动作。Build an ‘error culture’: categorize errors and pair each with a repair action.
  • 用更具体的反馈替代评价：从‘不认真’到‘你漏了变量定义’。Swap judgments for specific feedback: ‘not trying’ → ‘missing variable definition.’
  • 英文句型：“My prediction was ___, but the evidence shows ___.”English frame: “My prediction was ___, but the evidence shows ___.”
