Lesson 7：我们的大脑能创造现实 Lesson 7: Our Brains Can Create Reality

现实不仅是‘外界发生了什么’，也是大脑如何赋予意义、组织体验。 Reality is not only what happens ‘out there’—it’s how the brain assigns meaning and organizes experience.

概念卡Concept cards 讨论Discussion 练习Practice

Big Idea 核心概念Big Idea

当你改变解释框架、改变注意与语言，你就部分改变了你的现实体验。 When you change frames, attention, and language, you partly change your experienced reality.

Concept Cards 概念卡Concept Cards

  • 意义建构（Meaning-making）：把事件放进某种解释框架。Meaning-making (意义建构): Placing events into explanatory frames.
  • 知觉（Perception）：知觉受预测与注意影响。Perception (知觉): Perception is shaped by prediction and attention.
  • 重构（Reframing）：换一个角度组织经验。Reframing (重构): Reorganizing experience from a new angle.
  • 能动性（Agency）：主动选择回应方式。Agency (能动性): Choosing responses deliberately.

理解展开Explanation

这不是否认客观事实，而是承认：我们可以训练自己的解释与回应，从而影响情绪与行动的轨迹。 This doesn’t deny objective facts; it recognizes we can train our interpretations and responses, shaping emotion and action trajectories.

讨论问题Discussion

  • 你最近一次‘同一件事，不同解释导致不同情绪’是什么？Share a time when different interpretations of the same event led to different emotions.
  • 你常用的解释框架是‘威胁’还是‘挑战’？它如何影响你？Is your default frame ‘threat’ or ‘challenge’? How does it affect you?
  • 如果要给学生一个“现实重构工具”，你会给什么？What ‘reality reframing tool’ would you give students?

生活练习Practice

  • 写下一个困扰事件，用三种框架重写：威胁/挑战/好奇。Rewrite a troubling event in three frames: threat/challenge/curiosity.
  • 做 2 分钟“注意力切换”：把注意从评价转到数据（我做了什么）。Do a 2-minute attention shift: from judgment to data (what I did).
  • 用一句英文完成重构：“A more helpful story is ___.”Complete an English reframe: “A more helpful story is ___.”
小提示：先“只看中文”完成，再切“中英对照”对照表达，最后“只看英文”复述。 Tip: Try ZH-only → bilingual → EN-only for retelling and fluency.

教学应用（SEL × 学术）Teaching Application (SEL × Academics)

  • 把SEL策略写进学术任务：先命名情绪→再做最小一步→再复盘故事。Embed SEL into academics: name emotion → smallest step → reflect on story.
  • 让学生练“叙事修订”：同一经历写三版结尾（成长/连接/行动）。Practice narrative revision: write three endings (growth/connection/action).
  • 英文句型：“The facts are ___. My story was ___. I choose a better story: ___.”English frame: “The facts are ___. My story was ___. I choose a better story: ___.”
