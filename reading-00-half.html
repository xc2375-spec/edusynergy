Half Lesson：你的大脑不是为了思考 Half Lesson: Your Brain Is Not for Thinking

大脑首先用于调节身体与能量预算；思考只是其中一部分。 The brain primarily regulates the body and its energy budget; thinking is only part of the job.

概念卡Concept cards 讨论Discussion 练习Practice

Big Idea 核心概念Big Idea

大脑的第一职责是预测并调节身体需求（allostasis）。当身体预算紧张时，认知表现会自然下降。 The brain’s first responsibility is to predict and regulate bodily needs (allostasis). When the body budget is tight, cognition naturally drops.

Concept Cards 概念卡Concept Cards

  • 预调节（Allostasis）：提前预测并分配身体资源，而不是等问题发生才修补。Allostasis (预调节): Predictive regulation—allocating resources before problems arise.
  • 身体预算（Body budget）：睡眠、营养、压力、运动等共同构成可用能量。Body budget (身体预算): Available energy shaped by sleep, nutrition, stress, and movement.
  • 预测（Prediction）：大脑用预测来指导感知与行动。Prediction (预测): The brain uses prediction to guide perception and action.
  • 调节（Regulation）：让身体维持相对稳定的过程。Regulation (调节): Processes that keep the body relatively stable.

理解展开Explanation

这帮助我们把“学习状态不好”从道德评价（懒/不努力）转成系统视角：我现在的身体预算是否够？环境是否支持？ This reframes “poor learning” from moral judgment (lazy) to a systems view: is the body budget sufficient, and is the environment supportive?

讨论问题Discussion

  • 你在什么时候最“清醒好用”？那时你的睡眠/饮食/压力是什么样？When are you at your cognitive best? What’s your sleep/food/stress like then?
  • 当你焦虑或疲惫时，你更容易出现哪些学习错误？When anxious or tired, what mistakes increase for you?
  • 如果大脑要先保生存，老师/家长能做的第一件事是什么？If the brain prioritizes survival, what is the first thing adults can do?

生活练习Practice

  • 一周记录：每天给自己的身体预算打分（0–10），并写下影响因素（睡眠/运动/压力）。Track for one week: rate your body budget (0–10) and note drivers (sleep/movement/stress).
  • 在学习前做 90 秒“重置”：喝水、伸展、深呼吸，观察专注变化。Do a 90-second reset before studying: water, stretch, slow breath—notice focus change.
  • 把一次“走神/发脾气”当作信号：我缺的可能不是意志力，而是恢复。Treat distraction/meltdowns as signals: it may be recovery, not willpower, that’s missing.

教学应用（SEL × 学术）Teaching Application (SEL × Academics)

  • 给课堂加一个“状态入口”：上课前 30 秒自评能量（0–5），老师据此调整节奏。Add a ‘state check-in’: 30 seconds to rate energy (0–5) and adjust pace accordingly.
  • 把‘休息/补给/运动’视为学习策略的一部分，而非奖励或惩罚。Treat rest/fuel/movement as learning strategies, not rewards or punishments.
  • 教学生用一句英文表达状态：“My body budget is low today, so I need a short reset.”Teach an English self-advocacy line: “My body budget is low today, so I need a short reset.”
