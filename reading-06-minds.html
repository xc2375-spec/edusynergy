Lesson 6：大脑可以产生不止一种心智 Lesson 6: Brains Make More Than One Kind of Mind

‘心智’不是单一标准答案：文化、语言与训练会塑造不同的思维方式与注意习惯。 There isn’t one ‘standard mind.’ Culture, language, and training shape different ways of thinking and attending.

概念卡Concept cards 讨论Discussion 练习Practice

Big Idea 核心概念Big Idea

同一个人也会在不同场域切换心智：学生心智、孩子心智、领导心智——都是真实能力。 A person can switch minds across contexts: student mind, child mind, leader mind—these are real capacities.

Concept Cards 概念卡Concept Cards

  • 文化（Culture）：文化提供解释框架与价值优先级。Culture (文化): Culture provides frames and priorities.
  • 语言（Language）：语言影响分类与注意。Language (语言): Language shapes categorization and attention.
  • 规范（Norms）：什么被鼓励/惩罚塑造行为。Norms (规范): What’s rewarded/punished shapes behavior.
  • 灵活性（Flexibility）：在多种心智间切换的能力。Flexibility (灵活性): Ability to switch between minds.

理解展开Explanation

教育不是把孩子统一成一种‘标准人’，而是帮助他们发展可切换的心智与自我解释能力（metacognition）。 Education isn’t standardizing kids—it’s building flexible minds and metacognition (self-explanation).

讨论问题Discussion

  • 你在哪些场景更‘敢说’？哪些场景更‘谨慎’？这说明什么？Where are you more ‘outspoken’ vs ‘cautious’? What does that reveal?
  • 双语/跨文化经历给你带来了哪些‘心智切换’能力？What mind-switching did bilingual/cross-cultural life give you?
  • 学校如何尊重多种心智，同时保持学术标准？How can schools respect diverse minds while keeping academic standards?

生活练习Practice

  • 本周做一次‘心智切换’：同一主题分别用中文/英文解释一次。Do a mind-switch: explain the same topic once in Chinese and once in English.
  • 写下‘我在这个场域的自我定义’：我是怎样的学习者？Write ‘my identity in this context’: what kind of learner am I here?
  • 找一个文化差异点，做“好奇而不评判”的记录。Pick one cultural difference and record it with curiosity, not judgment.
小提示：先“只看中文”完成，再切“中英对照”对照表达，最后“只看英文”复述。 Tip: Try ZH-only → bilingual → EN-only for retelling and fluency.

教学应用（SEL × 学术）Teaching Application (SEL × Academics)

  • 课堂任务提供两种表达路径：结构化（框架）与创造性（例子）。Offer two expression pathways: structured (frames) and creative (examples).
  • 评估区分：概念理解 vs 表达风格（避免把风格当错误）。Assess concept understanding separately from style (don’t grade style as wrong).
  • 英文句型：“In my context, this idea means ___, because ___.”English frame: “In my context, this idea means ___, because ___.”
