春节聚餐：祝福话与待人接物 Spring Festival Meals: Blessings & Social Etiquette

不是背祝福话，而是练习：看见情境、表达关系、守住边界、稳定情绪。 Not memorizing lines—practicing: reading the room, expressing care, holding boundaries, and regulating emotions.

这在培养哪些能力？What skills does this build?

情境判断Situational awareness

看懂对象与场合，决定怎么说、说多少。Match your words to the person and the context—how much to say, and how.

关系表达Relational expression

把尊重、感谢与关心放进语言里。Put respect, gratitude, and care into language.

边界与调节Boundaries & regulation

面对追问与比较，礼貌回应、保护自尊。Respond politely to pressure, protect self-respect, stay steady.

被问成绩/排名：怎么礼貌回应？Asked about grades/rank: how to respond politely?

结构：事实 + 方向 + 礼貌收束Structure: fact + direction + polite close

  • “今年在努力把基础打牢，慢慢有进步，谢谢您关心。”“I’m strengthening my fundamentals this year and making steady progress—thanks for asking.”
  • “我在探索更适合自己的方法，最近在坚持做复盘。”“I’m finding methods that fit me better, and I’ve been reflecting regularly.”
  • “谢谢关心！您最近怎么样？”（轻轻转话题）“Thanks for caring—how have you been lately?” (gentle redirect)
被比较：别人家孩子更优秀？Compared to others: “Other kids are better”

目标：不对抗、不自贬、保持自尊。Goal: no fight, no self-put-down, keep dignity.

  • “TA 很厉害，我也在找到自己的节奏。”“They’re impressive. I’m also finding my own pace.”
  • “我更关注把自己的短板补起来，慢慢来。”“I’m focusing on improving my weaker areas—step by step.”
  • “谢谢提醒，我会记在心里。”（收束）“Thanks for the reminder—I’ll keep it in mind.” (close)
被夸奖：怎么接住不尴尬？Complimented: how to accept without awkwardness?

结构：感谢 + 归因努力/他人 + 轻谦Structure: thanks + effort/others + gentle humility

  • “谢谢您，我也在慢慢学，还需要多练。”“Thank you—I’m still learning and practicing.”
  • “谢谢！主要是最近更认真安排时间了。”“Thanks! I’ve been managing my time more carefully.”
  • “谢谢夸奖，您这么说我更有动力了。”“That’s kind of you—hearing this motivates me.”
不想聊天但要有礼貌：怎么“短而稳”？Don’t want to chat, but stay polite: short & steady?

结构：礼貌回应 + 一句话近况 + 结束语Structure: polite reply + one-line update + closing

  • “谢谢关心，我最近挺好的，祝您新年顺利！”“Thanks for asking—I’m doing fine. Wishing you a great new year!”
  • “我最近在忙学校的事，改天再跟您细聊。”“I’ve been busy with school—let’s catch up another time.”

亲子对话：三问就够（饭前 10 分钟）Family conversation: 3 prompts (10 minutes before the meal)

  1. 什么样的祝福你觉得真诚？（让孩子说“喜欢/不喜欢”的理由）What kind of blessing feels sincere to you? (Ask: why?)
  2. 什么问题最容易让你尴尬？（一起准备一句回应）Which questions feel awkward? (Craft one response together.)
  3. 如果你是主人，你希望客人怎么对你？（练角色转换）If you were the host, what would you want from guests? (Practice perspective-taking.)
小规则：不纠正表达风格，只纠正“是否尊重/是否有边界”。让孩子保留自己的声音。 Rule: Don’t correct “style”—only check respect and boundaries. Keep their voice.
