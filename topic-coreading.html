概念卡Concept cards 讨论Discussion 练习Practice

如何使用这个专栏？How to use this series

  1. 先读概念卡：抓住本期最重要的 3–5 个关键词。Start with concept cards: capture the 3–5 most important keywords.
  2. 再做讨论：用问题把“理解”变成“表达”。Then discuss: turn understanding into articulation.
  3. 最后做练习：把书里的观点放进真实生活一周。Finish with practice: apply the idea to real life for one week.
提示：如果给学生用，可以先“只看中文”，再切“中英”对照，最后“只看英文”复述。 Tip: For students, try “ZH only” → “BI” → “EN only” for retelling.
